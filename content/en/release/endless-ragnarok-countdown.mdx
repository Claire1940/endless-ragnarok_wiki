<Section icon={Swords} title="New Gameplay Systems: Master Traits & Summons">

Endless Ragnarok introduces two massive character progression systems that completely reshape the meta: **Master Traits** and **Summons**. These systems allow you to push your favorite skyfarers far beyond their previous limitations.

### Character Progression & Master Traits
Once a character reaches Level 100 and you have attained the **Fatebreaker** skyfarer grade, you can begin spending Mastery Points (MSP) to raise their Master Level. 
- **Equip Slots**: Higher Master Levels allow you to equip more Master Traits simultaneously.
- **Style Perks**: Equipping enough Master Traits belonging to a specific Master Style unlocks powerful, passive style perks that grant unique combat effects.

### Summoning Mechanics & Primal Bursts
Players can now equip up to **four summons** at once after completing the Summons tutorial.
- **Active Control**: Some summons, once channeled by Lyria, can be temporarily controlled directly by the player in battle.
- **Primal Bursts**: Meeting specific battle conditions allows your summons to trigger a devastating **Primal Burst** alongside your standard Chain Bursts.

| System | Unlock Requirement | Core Resource Needed | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Master Traits** | Level 100 + Fatebreaker Grade | Mastery Points (MSP) | Passive style perks & limit breaks |
| **Summons** | Fatebreaker Grade + Tutorial | Summon Slots (Max 4) | Active combat control & Primal Bursts |

<Callout type="success" title="Endgame Farming">
Use repeatable Chaos-level quests to farm the materials and MSP needed to max out your Master Levels and optimize your summon loadouts.
</Callout>
