<Section icon={Swords} title="New Gameplay Systems: Conflux and Master Traits">

Beyond the **Endless Ragnarok release time** and pricing, the expansion introduces transformative gameplay mechanics. The most significant addition is **The Conflux**, a solo-focused roguelite mode. In this mode, players navigate sequential zones filled with randomized puzzles and combat encounters. After each zone, you select temporary perks that only last for that specific run, allowing for incredibly powerful (and experimental) character builds.

Additionally, the **Master Traits** system provides a new layer of progression for every character. These traits allow you to push your favorite heroes beyond their previous power caps, specializing them into roles like pure DPS, unbreakable tanks, or ultimate support casters.

**Key Expansion Features:**
- **The Conflux**: A repeatable solo endgame mode with unique treasure rewards.
- **Master Traits**: Deep character customization to refine post-game builds.
- **Summon System**: Call upon powerful entities like Proto Bahamut in battle.
- **Chaos Quests**: The highest difficulty tier featuring the new Ragnalia enemy type.
- **Primal Bursts**: Devastating full-party attacks triggered by specific battle conditions.
