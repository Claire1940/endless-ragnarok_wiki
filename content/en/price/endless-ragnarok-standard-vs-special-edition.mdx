<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Deep Dive: Special Pack and DLC Collection Contents">

The primary differentiator in the **endless ragnarok standard vs special edition** comparison is the additional digital content. The Special Edition (and its corresponding Upgrade Kit) includes two major bundles that are not found in the Standard version.

**The Endless Ragnarok Special Pack**
This pack is focused on the expansion's new stars and the core trio. It includes:
- **Exclusive Colors**: Special color variants for the Captain (Gran/Djeeta), Id, and Sandalphon.
- **Unlocking Materials**: Essential items used to facilitate the recruitment of the Eternals (Seofon and Tweyen) and the Primarch Sandalphon.

**The Granblue Fantasy: Relink DLC Collection**
This is a legacy bundle that brings forward content from the original game's post-launch updates:
- **Color Packs 1-5**: Dozens of color variations for the entire base roster.
- **Emote Expansion Sets**: New social animations for online co-op play.
- **Item Packs**: Consumables for weapon upgrading and sigil refinement.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Pre-order and Early Purchase Bonuses">

Regardless of the **endless ragnarok standard vs special edition** choice, the timing of your purchase can net you additional rewards. Cygames has established different bonus windows for physical and digital copies in 2026.

**Physical Edition Rewards**
First-production physical copies of the Standard Edition include a voucher code for the **Guts III & Autorevive III Sigil Set**. These are vital defensive tools for the early-to-mid game, allowing you to survive lethal hits and automatically revive once per battle.

**Steam Early Purchase Bonus**
PC players who purchase any version of the expansion on Steam before August 31, 2026, receive the **Supplementary Damage V and War Elemental Sigil Set**.
- **Supplementary Damage V**: Grants a chance to deal additional damage on every hit, significantly boosting DPS.
- **War Elemental**: Changes the character's element to the enemy's weakness, a "must-have" for high-end endgame builds.
