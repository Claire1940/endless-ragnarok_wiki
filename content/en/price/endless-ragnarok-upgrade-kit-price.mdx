<Section icon={Shield} title="Platform Availability and Requirements">

The *Endless Ragnarok* expansion launches across four major platforms, bringing robust crossplay features that unite the community. However, storage and subscription requirements vary slightly between consoles and PC.

### Storage Requirements
- **Nintendo Switch 2**: Requires a digital download of **40GB or more**. Physical versions on the Switch 2 are distributed as game-key cards rather than traditional cartridges, meaning an active internet connection is mandatory to retrieve the game files.
- **PlayStation & PC**: Ensure you have at least **90GB of free space** allocated to accommodate the high-fidelity textures and massive new zones.

### Multiplayer Requirements
- **PlayStation 5 / PlayStation 4**: Online co-op matchmaking requires an active PlayStation Plus subscription.
- **Nintendo Switch 2**: Online co-op matchmaking requires an active Nintendo Switch Online (NSO) membership.
- **PC (Steam)**: Online multiplayer is completely free with no additional subscription services required.
