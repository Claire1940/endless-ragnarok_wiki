<Section icon={Target} title="The Conflux: Solo Endgame Progression Guide">

One of the most praised additions in the **endless ragnarok special edition** is **The Conflux**. This mode addresses the community's desire for a robust single-player experience that rivals the intensity of online co-op. It functions as a roguelite "gauntlet" where players navigate randomized zones, collecting temporary buffs to tackle increasingly difficult floor bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Requirements">
    Complete the main "Endless Ragnarok" story arc and reach Skyfarer Rank "Zegagrande Hero" to unlock the Conflux portal in the Seedhollow hub.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Zone Navigation">
    Enter the zones alone. You will face a mix of puzzles, horde battles, and Ragnalia mini-bosses. Each zone clear grants a choice of three randomized perks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Perk Synergy">
    Focus on perks that enhance your specific build. For example, if playing Narmaya, prioritize "Skill Cooldown Reduction" and "Critical Hit Damage Expansion."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boss Encounters">
    Every five floors, you face a major boss. These encounters reward "Conflux Treasure," which is the only currency used to unlock the new **Master Traits**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Run Termination">
If you fall in battle within The Conflux, you lose all temporary perks and current floor progress. However, you retain all "Conflux Treasure" collected up to that point. Always prioritize survival perks like "Life on Kill" during the early floors of a run.
</Callout>

Master Traits represent the final layer of character progression. These are passive nodes that push a character's stats beyond the previous 100% cap, allowing for "Damage Cap" breaks that were previously impossible. This system ensures that even after 200 hours of play, there is still meaningful power to gain.

</Section>
