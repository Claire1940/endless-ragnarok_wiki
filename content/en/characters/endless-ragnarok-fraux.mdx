<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Fraux's Core Mechanics & Combat Flow">

The core of **endless ragnarok fraux**'s gameplay revolves around her interactions with **The Devil** and her ability to manipulate skill cooldowns. Understanding her combo loop is essential to maximizing her damage output in high-tier Chaos quests.

### The Zero-Cooldown Loop
Fraux can enter a enhanced state where she can chain up to **three skills consecutively with zero cooldown**. 
- **Skill Damage Scaling**: Each skill used during this chain increases her overall skill damage and raises her damage cap.
- **Dimensional Strikes**: Her standard combo strings open dimensional portals, summoning massive fists that deal heavy stun and break damage to bosses.
- **Positioning**: She features excellent forward momentum on her attacks, allowing her to stick to mobile targets easily.

| Combat State | Damage Modifier | Cooldown Penalty | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard State** | Baseline | Normal Cooldowns | Building gauge, repositioning |
| **Devil Alignment** | +20% Skill Damage | 50% Cooldown Reduction | Burst prep, clearing trash mobs |
| **Zero-Cooldown Loop** | Max Damage Cap | **No Cooldown** (3 Skills) | Boss stagger windows, full burst |

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Buff Durations">
The window to chain your zero-cooldown skills is brief. If you get interrupted or knocked back by a boss's EX Burst attack, you will lose the chain and put all skills on their standard cooldowns.
</Callout>
