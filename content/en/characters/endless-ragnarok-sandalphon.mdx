### Step-by-Step Unlocking Guide

Follow these steps to recruit Sandalphon and claim his exclusive equipment:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Crewmate Cards">
    Earn two normal crewmate cards by progressing through the main story and clearing high-level side quests. Alternatively, purchase the Special Edition of the game to bypass this requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit Sierokarte">
    Head to Sierokarte's Knickknack Shack in Folca or Seedhollow. Select the character recruitment menu and exchange your cards to unlock Sandalphon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Add-on Items">
    If you purchased a bundle containing the **Endless Ragnarok Special Pack**, talk to Sierokarte and select **Claim Add-Ons** to receive his unique concept art colors and upgrade materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Duplicate Purchase Warning">
Ensure you do not purchase the standalone character packs if you already plan to buy the Special Edition or Special Upgrade Kit, as these bundles already include Sandalphon and his progression items.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Skills and Combos">

Sandalphon's kit revolves around his ability to switch between standard combat and his enhanced **Chromatic Wings** state. Understanding his skill interactions is key to maintaining high damage uptime.

### Core Skill Analysis

- **Talviyö**: His most important utility skill. Landing the final blow instantly fills his Archangel gauge.
- **Zohar Strike**: A heavy-hitting finisher. Using this skill deals incredible damage but immediately ends the Chromatic Wings state, so it should only be used when his gauge is nearly empty.
- **Righteous Deed**: Enhances his perfectly timed primary attacks, rewarding precise button presses with massive damage scaling.

### Recommended Skill Loadouts

Depending on your party composition and the difficulty of the quest, adjust Sandalphon's active skills using the configurations below:

| Build Style | Skill 1 | Skill 2 | Skill 3 | Skill 4 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pure Burst DPS** | Talviyö | Zohar Strike | Righteous Deed | Offensive Buff / Damage Skill |
| **Balanced Utility** | Talviyö | Righteous Deed | Defensive / Healing Skill | Crowd Control / Support Skill |

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanic Explanation">
While Chromatic Wings are active, Sandalphon's base skills change. They retain their basic utility but gain significantly increased damage, wider areas of effect, and unique elemental properties.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Endgame Build Setup">

To take Sandalphon into Chaos-difficulty quests or the deep cycles of **The Conflux**, you must optimize his sigils and Master Traits.

### Essential Sigils and Master Traits

- **Supplementary Damage V**: Provides a flat percentage chance to deal extra damage on every hit, scaling exceptionally well with Sandalphon's rapid attacks.
- **War Elemental**: Converts all of his attacks into the elemental weakness of the target, ensuring maximum damage output regardless of the boss's native element.
- **Master Traits**: Unlock these once Sandalphon reaches level 100 and you attain the skyfarer grade of **Fatebreaker**. Prioritize offensive nodes that boost his Archangel gauge gain rate.
