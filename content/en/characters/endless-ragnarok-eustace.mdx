<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics & Bullet Management">

The core of Eustace's gameplay revolves around managing his unique ammunition system. Unlike standard ranged attackers, Eustace uses lightning-infused bullets that must be monitored closely to maintain peak damage output. Every shot fired alters his positioning, allowing for active repositioning during intense boss encounters.

<Callout type="warning" title="Ammunition Warning">
Firing all 5 bullets triggers an automatic reload animation. During this animation, Eustace is highly vulnerable to incoming attacks. Always plan your reloads when the enemy is recovering or distracted by your teammates.
</Callout>

Each sequence consists of five distinct lightning bullets. The fifth bullet in the sequence is automatically empowered, dealing significantly higher damage and applying elemental pressure. Players can also charge their bullets to modify their properties, expanding their combat options.

| Bullet Type | Charging Mechanic | Combat Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Shot | No charge required | Fast single-target damage |
| Empowered Shot | Automatic on 5th bullet | High damage with moderate impact |
| Charged Bullet | Hold attack button | Extended range and increased stagger |
| AoE Charged Shot | Fully charge 5th bullet | Wide-area lightning explosion |

In addition to basic shots, Eustace features a powerful counter-attack mechanic. Executing a perfect dodge immediately triggers a special counter-bullet that deals area-of-effect damage around the target. This rewards defensive play and keeps his damage output high even when forced to play defensively.

</Section>
