<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Delta Clock & Devour Causality Mechanics">

Beatrix's combat design revolves around her unique gauge: the **Delta Clock**. By pressing or holding her unique action button, players can rotate the clock hand to activate three distinct states. Landing combo finishers charges her Embrasque gauge, leading to her ultimate combat state.

### Delta Clock Buff Positions

The Delta Clock provides three distinct buffs depending on where the pointer is set. 

| Clock Position | Mode | Combat Effects |
|---|---|---|
| **Top (Red)** | Attack Mode | Grants significant attack power and raw damage multipliers. |
| **Right (Green)** | Healing Mode | Restores health over time and boosts incoming healing effects. |
| **Left (Yellow)** | Defense Mode | Increases defense and provides valuable stagger resistance. |

### Devour Causality Mode

Once the circular Embrasque gauge is completely filled via combo finishers, holding the Delta Clock button triggers **Devour Causality**. During this overloaded state:
- Attack speed is massively increased, allowing you to cycle combos rapidly.
- Combo finishers chain into upgraded, multi-hit follow-up attacks.
- Dodging immediately after a combo finisher will no longer interrupt your ongoing combo string, allowing for unmatched offensive uptime.

<Callout type="warning" title="Vulnerability Warning">
Beatrix does not possess default invincibility frames (i-frames) during her long combo finisher animations. You must rely on perfect dodges or defensive sigils to avoid getting interrupted by hard-hitting bosses.
</Callout>
