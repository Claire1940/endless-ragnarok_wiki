<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Combat Mechanics and Abilities">

Facing **endless ragnarok lucilius** requires a deep understanding of his attack patterns. Unlike standard bosses, Lucilius utilizes a "Labor" system that imposes penalties on the players if specific conditions are not met. His attacks are fast, cover large areas, and often inflict multiple status ailments like Skill Sealed or SBA Sealed.

| Move Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Paradise Lost** | Full-screen ultimate attack with a long charge time | Must use Skybound Arts (SBA) or reach Link Time |
| **Iblis** | Multiple purple orbs that track players and explode | Continuous movement and Perfect Dodge timing |
| **Phosphorus** | A high-speed linear beam targeting the furthest player | Side-step immediately when the red line appears |
| **The Word** | A series of circular AOE blasts around the arena | Stay close to the boss or find the safe gaps |
| **Seven Trumpets** | Transition phase that summons pillars of light | Destroy the resonance crystals within the time limit |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies and Team Synergy">

Surviving **endless ragnarok lucilius** is impossible without a coordinated team. Each member must know their role, especially when the "Twelve Labors" begin. If the party fails to clear these labors, Lucilius gains permanent attack buffs and reduces the party's maximum HP for the remainder of the fight.

| Role | Recommended Characters | Key Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Main DPS** | Narmaya, Zeta, Lancelot | Constant pressure and breaking the boss's guard |
| **Support/Heal** | Cagliostro, Gran/Djeeta | Maintaining buffs and reviving fallen allies |
| **Tank/Utility** | Siegfried, Vane | Providing 100% Damage Cut during ultimate attacks |
| **Aerial DPS** | Ferry, Rackam | Attacking during phases where the floor is covered in AOE |

To maximize your chances, consider the following tactical approach:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Link Gauge">
    Use characters with high hit counts to trigger Link Attacks. Reaching 100% Link Time is the only way to safely bypass some of Lucilius's faster mechanics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="SBA Management">
    Do not use your Skybound Arts individually. Always aim for a **Full Burst** to lock the boss in place and cancel his "Paradise Lost" countdown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Labor Priority">
    Focus on the "Elemental" labors first. These usually require hitting the boss with specific types of damage or interacting with spawned orbs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
Communication is key. If playing in multiplayer, use the preset stickers to coordinate SBA usage. Holding an SBA for even 5 seconds can mean the difference between life and death.
</Callout>

</Section>
