### The Supreme Weapon Essence Farming Route
If you choose to farm for Tweyen manually, you must progress through the main campaign until you unlock **Proud Difficulty** at the Quest Counter. Once Proud difficulty is unlocked, use the following steps:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Proud Difficulty">
    Complete the main story and clear the required key quests at the Quest Counter until the Proud tier is accessible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Behemoth Quest">
    Select the quest **"Lock Horns"** on Proud difficulty. This fight features the Behemoth and has a high drop rate for Supreme Weapon Essences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Revenant Medallions">
    If you struggle to get direct Essence drops, run quests that reward **Revenant Medallions**. Trade these at Siero's Knicknack Shack at a 30:1 ratio for Essences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Your Crewmate Card">
    Once you have 3 Supreme Weapon Essences, navigate to the Treasure Trade menu at the Knicknack Shack to claim Tweyen's Crewmate Card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Playing Tweyen effectively requires maintaining a safe distance while keeping up a constant stream of charged normal attacks and skill activations.

### The Multilock Loop
Tweyen's primary damage loop revolves around her charged shots:
1. Charge your normal attack to its maximum level and release.
2. Immediately hold down the charge button again to transition directly into **Multilock Hail**.
3. Use the adjusted camera settings to aim the barrage at the boss's weak points.
4. Cancel the recovery animation with a dodge if the boss targets your position.

### Boss Matchup: Seofon & Tweyen Trial
During your journey, you will encounter a challenging dual boss fight against **Seofon and Tweyen**. This fight serves as a major gateway trial to prove your party's readiness for the endgame.

| Boss Name | Element | Weakness | Threat Level | Key Attacks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Seofon & Tweyen** | Wind / Light | Fire | High | Dual Skybound Arts, Multilock Rain, Sword Barrage |

To defeat them, focus on taking down Tweyen first to eliminate her long-range tracking shots and crowd-control arrows. Bring Fire-element characters like Percival or Zeta to exploit their elemental weakness and break their guard quickly.
