**The Conflux** is a dedicated single-player endgame activity added in Endless Ragnarok [1]. It blends puzzles, sequential battle zones, and randomized perks into a repeatable roguelite farming route [1].

### Key Mechanics of The Conflux:
- **Solo Progression**: You must run this mode alone, making it the ultimate test of your individual build and skill [1].
- **Sequential Zones**: Clear rooms by completing specific objectives, such as defeating waves of enemies or solving movement puzzles [1].
- **Randomized Perks**: After clearing a zone, choose temporary buffs that boost your attack, defense, or summon cooldowns for the rest of the run [1].
- **High-Value Rewards**: Clearing deeper stages of The Conflux rewards rare treasures and materials needed to upgrade your Master Traits [1].
