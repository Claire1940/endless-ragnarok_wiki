<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Builds, Master Traits, and Sigil Setup">

Progression in Endless Ragnarok introduces **Master Traits**, a brand-new endgame layer designed to push your characters far beyond their previous limits. Rather than building characters uniformly, you must specialize them for specific roles.

### Understanding Master Traits
Master Traits act as a long-term post-game progression system. They allow you to unlock unique passive bonuses that complement a character's core identity. For example, tank characters can unlock higher damage mitigation, while damage dealers can further bypass damage caps.

### Sigil and Weapon Optimization
Your build's strength relies heavily on equipping the right combination of Sigils. In the expansion, balancing offensive power with defensive utility is vital for surviving Chaos-tier quests.

| Sigil Type | Primary Effect | Strategy Tip |
|---|---|---|
| **Supplementary Damage V** | Grants a chance to deal extra damage | Essential for reaching maximum DPS output |
| **War Elemental** | Changes all attacks to the enemy's weak element | Always slot this when fighting high-end bosses |
| **Guts / Autorevive** | Prevents one-shots and automatically revives you | Crucial for survival in multiplayer and solo play |
| **Damage Cap** | Increases the maximum damage your skills can deal | Prioritize upgrading this in the post-game |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid DPS-Only Builds">
While maximizing damage is tempting, pure DPS builds will struggle in Chaos quests. Always slot at least one defensive sigil like Guts or Autorevive to avoid failing quest attempt limits.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Conflux Mode and Endgame Milestones">

The Conflux is Endless Ragnarok's signature solo-focused endgame activity. It features randomized objectives, temporary run-only perks, and highly valuable progression rewards, making it the premier destination for solo players.

### Conflux Mode Mechanics
- **Sequential Zones**: Clear rooms filled with varying combinations of monsters, puzzles, and bosses.
- **Randomized Perks**: After clearing a zone, choose temporary buffs (e.g., increased summon damage, skill cooldown reduction) that last for the duration of the run.
- **Treasure Farming**: Successful runs yield exclusive materials required to upgrade weapons and unlock high-tier Master Traits.
