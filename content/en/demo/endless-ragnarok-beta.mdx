Post-game character building in Endless Ragnarok introduces deep customization layers. Players can now equip powerful summons to use in combat and unlock Master Traits to specialize their characters.

Master Traits act as a long-term progression system. Rather than building every character the same way, use Master Traits to turn them into specialized combat experts. Pair these traits with powerful sigils like Supplementary Damage V and War Elemental to maximize your damage output.

Summons provide a new layer of tactical depth. They can be equipped and called to perform unique attacks or provide defensive support during tough battles.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Conflux Solo Mode Guide">

The Conflux is Endless Ragnarok's signature single-player endgame activity. This mode is designed for players who want to test their individual skills and farm valuable upgrade materials without relying on online matchmaking.

### Mode Structure
Runs in The Conflux are structured as sequential zones containing a mix of intense battles and puzzles. As you clear zones, you will earn temporary perks and special buffs that last for the duration of your run.

### Farming Strategy
- **Prioritize Survival**: Choose temporary perks that boost your HP recovery and defense in early zones.
- **Adapt to Hazards**: Prepare a flexible build that can handle both single-target bosses and large groups of enemies.
- **Repeat for Rewards**: Use successful runs to accumulate rare treasure, which can be spent to enhance your weapons and unlock Master Traits.

<Callout type="tip" title="Run-Only Powers">
