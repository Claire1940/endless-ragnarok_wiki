### Master Traits and Summons

Master Traits serve as the ultimate post-game progression layer, allowing you to specialize characters beyond their standard skill caps. Additionally, the new summon system allows Lyria to channel powerful entities directly onto the battlefield.

- **Primal Bursts**: Activating Skybound Arts with all four party members while a summon is active triggers a devastating Primal Burst, summoning legendary figures like Proto Bahamut to obliterate targets.
- **EX Bursts**: Late-game bosses emit a distinct purple aura before executing EX Burst attacks. Use these visual cues to prepare your dodges, defensive buffs, or summon activation windows.

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Strategy">
Do not spread your resources too thin. Focus on fully upgrading one main damage dealer and two supportive AI companions first. This ensures you can comfortably clear solo content while remaining a valuable asset in online co-op.
</Callout>
