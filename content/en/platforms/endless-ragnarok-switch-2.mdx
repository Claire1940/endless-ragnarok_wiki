**Key Features of The Conflux:**
- **Randomized Perks**: After every zone, you choose a temporary buff that lasts for the duration of the run.
- **Sequential Zones**: Each room presents a different challenge, from boss rushes to environmental puzzles.
- **Solo Exclusive**: You cannot bring online friends into The Conflux; it is a true test of your individual character builds.
