Redeeming your retail insert codes for the physical edition requires a few straightforward steps. Follow this sequence to claim your items on your console profile.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unbox and Locate the Voucher">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Redeem on Platform Store">

  <Step num="3" title="Launch the Game and Load Save">

  </Step>
