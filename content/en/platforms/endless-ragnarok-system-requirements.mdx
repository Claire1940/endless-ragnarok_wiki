<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimization Steps for Lower-End PCs">

If your hardware sits right on the edge of the minimum **endless ragnarok system requirements**, you can still achieve stable performance by adjusting key in-game settings. Follow these steps to maximize your framerate without sacrificing too much visual clarity.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Adjust the Graphics Preset">
    Navigate to the main menu graphics options and change the overall preset to **Standard**. This automatically scales down resource-heavy settings like shadow quality and volumetric fog.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Limit the Framerate Target">
    Set your in-game framerate cap to **30 FPS** or **60 FPS** depending on your GPU capabilities. Uncapped framerates can cause severe thermal throttling on older graphics cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reduce V-Sync and Motion Blur">
    Turn off Motion Blur entirely to free up processing overhead. Enable V-Sync only if you experience screen tearing; otherwise, keep it disabled to minimize input latency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Background Applications">
    Close demanding web browsers, overlays, and recording software before launching the game. This ensures all 16 GB of your system RAM is dedicated to rendering the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Summon Mechanic Performance">
The newly introduced Summon mechanic features highly detailed visual effects and particle systems. If you experience lag during Primal Bursts, lowering the **VFX Quality** setting in the graphics menu will immediately stabilize your framerate.
</Callout>
