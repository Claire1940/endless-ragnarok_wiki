<Section icon={Zap} title="Master Traits and Build Optimization">

Character progression in **endless ragnarok steam** has been deepened through the Master Traits system. These are not merely stat increases; they are transformative upgrades that can change how a character's skills function. For example, a Master Trait might add an elemental explosion to a standard melee finisher or provide a massive cooldown reduction when a perfect dodge is performed.

Pairing these traits with the right Sigils is essential for tackling the endgame. Rare Sigils such as "War Elemental" allow your character to always deal damage with the elemental advantage, regardless of the enemy's actual weakness. This is a game-changer for the new Ragnalia bosses, who often shift their elemental resistances mid-fight.

| Build Component | Function | Recommendation |
